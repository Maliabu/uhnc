Maternal Health

Pregnant Mothers

Maintaining Good Nutrition and a healthy diet during pregnancy is crucial for the health of both the mother and the unborn child. Therefore, UHNC is really concerned and we are here to provide you with nutritional education and counselling to improve the nutritional status of women during pregnancy.

Our information is mainly focusing on:

  • Promote a healthy diet by increasing the diversity and amount of foods consumed
  • Promote adequate weight gain through sufficient and balanced protein and energy intake.
  • Promoting consistent and continued use of micronutrient supplements, food supplements or fortified foods

Nutrition

As we all may know, as a pregnant woman, your body goes through lots of physical and hormonal changes, so to fuel yourself and the growing baby, you will need to choose from varieties of the sources. Remember, the food you eat is your baby’s main source of nourishment And guess the good news, all these nutrition guide lines are not hard to follow
